About The Project

An AI-powered truth detection platform that combines Groq AI, local Ollama fallback, and trusted external sources to instantly verify news authenticity — built for speed, accuracy, and real-world impact.

The Challenge

Misinformation spreads faster than ever. Every second, false narratives flood social media, manipulate public opinion, and erode trust in legitimate journalism. Traditional fact-checking is too slow — by the time a claim is verified, it has already reached millions.

We built an AI that flips the equation — verifying news in real-time, at scale, with transparent reasoning that anyone can understand.

Our Mission

Deliver real-time fake news detection with sub-5 second response times
Combine LLM-powered reasoning with trusted source evidence for cross-validated results
Provide clear AI explanations so users understand why news is flagged real or fake
Support both text analysis and image OCR for versatile news verification
Maintain privacy with local Phi-3 LLM fallback when cloud APIs are unavailable

How It Works

Three-layer intelligence pipeline that delivers results in seconds

1

Trusted Sources

Searches trusted news and fact-check sources related to the entered claim, then uses those references as supporting evidence.

2

AI Inference

Groq cloud AI (Llama 3.3 70B) provides fast, intelligent cross-validation. Falls back to local Phi-3 via Ollama when needed.

3

Result Engine

Combines AI verdicts, confidence scoring, external fact checks, sentiment analysis, and keyword extraction into a clear explainable result.
